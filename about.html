Our Story

Born from a Broken Heart, Built with Compassion

In 2012, our founder Dr. Anita Sharma watched a 4-year-old girl named Meena die waiting for a heart surgery her family couldn't afford. The surgery would have cost ₹1.8 lakhs — a sum raised in just 3 days when word spread on social media.

Meena died two days before the surgery could happen. That day, Dr. Sharma promised she would build a system so no child would ever lose their life to poverty again. HealHope Foundation was born.

Today, we operate across 22 states in India with a network of 180+ partner hospitals, 600+ volunteer doctors, and over 85,000 donors who collectively fund life-saving treatment for children every single day.

Our Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

  • Ensure no child dies due to inability to afford medical care
  • Build transparent, accountable fund management systems
  • Connect donors directly with verified medical cases
  • Support families through entire treatment and recovery
  • Provide 80G tax certification to every donor
🌟

Our Vision

  • A world where a child's survival never depends on wealth
  • 1 million children helped by 2030 across South Asia
  • A network of 10,000 volunteer doctors across 50 countries
  • Zero administrative overhead — 100% donor-funded
  • Fully digital, real-time impact tracking for every donor
What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🔍

Transparency

Every rupee is tracked and reported publicly. Donors receive real-time case updates, expense breakdowns, and annual audited reports without ever asking.

💙

Compassion

We treat every child and family with dignity. Our volunteers go beyond medicine — they provide emotional support, regular check-ins, and long-term follow-up care.

⚡

Speed

When a child's life is at risk, minutes matter. Our emergency response team can mobilise funds and connect with hospitals within 24–48 hours of case verification.

🤝

Integrity

We turn away no case that meets our criteria, regardless of religion, caste, region, or background. Every verified child gets equal access to our resources.

📊

Accountability

We publish quarterly impact reports, complete financial audits, and individual case outcome reports. Our books are open to every donor, always.

🌱

Sustainability

We build long-term relationships with partner hospitals and recurring donors to create a sustainable funding ecosystem that children can rely on for years.
